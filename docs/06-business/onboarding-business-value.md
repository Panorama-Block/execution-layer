# Onboarding Business Value

## The Activation Problem

PanoramaBlock combines chat agents, a MiniApp, wallet connections, and multiple DeFi operations. Without clear onboarding, users:
- Don't understand the difference between chat and MiniApp
- Don't know how to connect a wallet
- Don't know what actions are available
- Abandon before first action

## Impact on Metrics

### Activation Rate
Users who complete onboarding are significantly more likely to perform their first DeFi action. Guided onboarding reduces the gap between "signed up" and "took first action."

### Retention
Users who understand the product return. Users who are confused churn. Clear onboarding explains value before asking for commitment.

### Time to First Action
Shorter time to first action correlates with higher retention. Guided flows remove friction (wallet connection, chain selection, token discovery).

## Content Strategy

Key content blocks needed:
1. What is PanoramaBlock (30 seconds to understand)
2. How it works (chat to execution flow)
3. Connect your wallet (step by step)
4. Your first action (guided swap or stake)
5. What happens next (ongoing value)

## Success Metrics

- Onboarding completion rate (% of new users who finish onboarding)
- Time to first action (minutes from first visit to first transaction)
- First-week retention (% of activated users who return within 7 days)
- Wallet connection rate (% of visitors who connect a wallet)

## The 10,000 User Opportunity

10,000 Avalanche users are waiting for activation. Better onboarding directly translates to more activated users and more on-chain activity, which strengthens the grant/ecosystem narrative.
