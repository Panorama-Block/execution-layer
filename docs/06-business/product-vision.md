# Product Vision

## One-Line Vision

PanoramaBlock is an AI-native execution infrastructure for decentralized finance.

## Three Pillars

### 1. AI Agents
Copilot-style DeFi interface. Users express goals in natural language. Agents interpret, plan, and guide execution.

### 2. On-Chain Execution
Smart contracts that execute DeFi operations through PanoramaBlock infrastructure. Real on-chain activity, measurable ecosystem value.

### 3. Multi-Protocol Strategies
Automated composition of operations across multiple protocols and chains. Swap, lend, provide liquidity, and stake in coordinated strategies.

## Target Users

- DeFi newcomers who want guidance (chat-first interaction)
- Experienced users who want efficiency (strategy automation)
- Portfolio managers who want intelligence (multi-protocol optimization)

## Market Positioning

PanoramaBlock creates a new category: AI-native DeFi orchestration.

Unlike wallets (key management), dashboards (analytics), or protocol UIs (single-protocol interaction), PanoramaBlock coordinates across the entire DeFi stack.

## Competitive Advantage

- Intent-based interaction (not protocol-based)
- Multi-chain execution infrastructure (not chain-specific)
- Strategy intelligence (not just routing)
- Telegram-native distribution (existing user channel)
- Reusable infrastructure (deploy once, use everywhere)
